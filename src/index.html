About our Restaurant:

Our restaurant has been funded by Kamil Toboła in the 1976, in Wrocław. As an avid traveler, who's visited more than 40 countries
and has eaten and enjoyed many famous cuisines. But it was the Georgian kitchen, who he loved and loves to this day, the most.
Therefore, when he returned to his hometown, Wrocław, he wanted everyone to experience the beautiful food of our european friends.
